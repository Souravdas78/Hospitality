WHAT IS LAHA?

Established for over 40 years, we are a coordinated network of pediatric pulmonary, immunology, and allergy physicians and supporting healthcare groups focused on innovation in patient care to exceed quality standards.

In affiliation with Long Beach Memorial Medical Center/Miller Children’s Hospital, we use a multidisciplinary approach to target the needs of all our patients and maintain ongoing quality care for many allergy and lung related diseases. Through our inclusive networks, we hope to advance treatment protocols in patient care to help our patients today.
